Fried Meat Empanadas

Description

This recipe is for Argentinian Fried Meat Empanadas but you can use any type of meat you want. They are excelent to enjoy with family or friends and a good wine.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of dough discs (15 units)
  • 500 grs. of ground beef
  • 2 diced onions
  • 1 diced spring onion
  • a cup of sliced green olives
  • 2 sliced hard-boiled eggs
  • a scoop of sweet paprika
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • frying oil

Steps

  1. In a pot, spray a little of olive oil and cook the diced onions

  2. Add the grounded beef and cook for 10 minutes

  3. Then add the salt, pepper and sweet paprika and mix

  4. Let the ingredients cool and add the olives and the sliced hard-boiled eggs, then mix

  5. In the center of a dough disc, put a scoop of the preparation

  6. Wet a finger in water and pass it in the edges of the disc and fold the extremes

  7. With the help of a fork, close the edges together

  8. Fry the empanadas in oil until they get a golden colour

  9. Withdraw on paper towels
